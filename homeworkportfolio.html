Week 8

Festival of Economics

The topic of the talk I attended at the Festival of Economics was 'Prices and Wages', where speakers discussed how inflation has reached an all time high, but that the prices of individual components that make up this indicator vary at significantly different rates. The chart below is supporting evidence of how much prices have increased in recent years as given by the Consumer Price Index (CPI).

The chart below looks at one component that makes up the CPI, Milk, Cheese and Eggs. Comparing the 2020-2023 period in the two charts gives one supporting example of how particular essentials have seen their average price rise much faster in recent times than the average rate of prices across the wider economy.
